※ If using a washing machine, complete steps 【1】 and 【2】 first, then place the tabi in a laundry net and select the hand wash setting. Avoid using the spin cycle. (It may cause shrinkage and deformation.)

### How to Dry Tabi

When drying, while the tabi are still damp, follow the steps 【1】 to 【6】 below and air-dry them with the front side facing up.

※ If the sole of the tabi is not stretched well before drying, the tabi may shrink and become difficult to wear. Therefore, always make sure to fully stretch the sole before drying.

### How to Iron Tabi

The tabi we recommend naturally stretch out wrinkles when worn, so ironing is generally unnecessary. However, for 100% cotton white tabi, if wrinkles are a concern, please iron them as described below. Set the iron to a high temperature and work quickly.
