※If using a washing machine, complete steps 【1】+【2】, then place the tabi socks in a laundry net and select the hand wash setting. Avoid using the spin cycle. (It may cause shrinkage and deformation.)

### How to Dry Tabi Socks

When drying, please follow steps 【1】～【6】 below while the tabi socks are still damp, keeping the front side up and drying in the shade.

※If the sole of the tabi socks is not stretched well before drying, it may shrink and become difficult to wear. Therefore, please make it a habit to thoroughly stretch the sole before drying.

### How to Iron Tabi Socks

The tabi socks we recommend naturally stretch out wrinkles when worn, so ironing is generally unnecessary. However, if wrinkles on 100% cotton white tabi socks bother you, please follow the steps below to iron them. Set the iron to a high temperature and iron quickly.
